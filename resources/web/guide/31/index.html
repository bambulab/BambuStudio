Help improve 3D printing for everyone by joining our User
Experience Improvement Program.
By learning from real prints, successes and failures, Bambu Studio can improve print quality, reliability, and overall performance.

If you join our program, your printer and software will share your usage data and performance data, such as device information, settings, connectivity, and print behavior. This helps us identify issues faster and improve our products.

Participation is optional, and you can leave at any time in Preferences > User Experience. For more details, visit the User Experience Improvement Program page.
